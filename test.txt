Different Types of LLMs and Their Differences
Encoder-Only Models (BERT)
Used for text understanding and classification.
Cannot generate long text effectively.
Decoder-Only Models (GPT, Llama)
Generate text by predicting the next word.
Used in chatbots, content generation, and coding assistants.
Encoder-Decoder Models (T5, BART)
Use an encoder to understand input and a decoder to generate output.
Best for translation, summarization, and question answering.
